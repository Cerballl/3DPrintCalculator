## Features

- **Accurate Costing:** Calculates total price based on filament cost, print time, and electricity rates.
- **Smart Markup:** Add your custom profit margin percentage for selling prints.
- **Built-in History:** Automatically saves your past calculations locally (`print_history.json`).
- **Modern UI:** Built with `customtkinter` for a beautiful, responsive dark-mode interface.
- **Portable:** Available as a standalone `.exe` file—no Python installation required for end-users.

---

## Example Calculation

Imagine you are printing a custom PETG DAC stand on a Bambu Lab A1 Mini:
* **Part Weight:** 85g
* **Print Time:** 2.5 hours
* **Filament Price:** 650 UAH/kg
* **Electricity:** 0.08 kW at 4.32 UAH/kWh
* **Markup:** 50%

The calculator will instantly process the material cost, machine time, and power consumption, giving you the exact base cost and final selling price for your Telegram store or clients.
